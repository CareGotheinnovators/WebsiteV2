Built in collaboration with the people who use it most, Themis-Trace is a next-generation inventory management system designed for real-world operations. Re-imagined from the ground up, it features a modern, intuitive interface, streamlined workflows, and powerful insights that transform how inventory is tracked, moved, and understood.

With a browser app designed around user workflows, Themis-Trace puts total control at your fingertips. Schedule shipments and receiving, print BOLs, and access detailed reports—all while maintaining accurate inventory records.

In a sector where traditional, manual systems are still prevalent, Themis-Trace brings clarity, confidence, and efficiency to every stage of the process.

Product Details

With Themis-Trace, users gain complete visibility into every inventory item. This view showcases essential product information including its current facility, storage location, QA status, associated shipping and receiving order IDs, bill of lading (BOL), and detailed billing and shipping information. Whether tracking where it's stored, who it's billed to, or when it was received—Themis-Trace ensures nothing goes unseen.

Streamlined Inventory Entry

Adding new inventory is simple and precise with the New Receiving Order screen. Users can quickly input detailed product specifications, such as gauge, dimensions, weight, and diameters—all in one place. The intuitive layout ensures accurate data capture while guiding users step-by-step through the receiving process, making it easy to bring products into the system with confidence.

Fast, Filterable Shipping Insights

The Shipping Grid gives users a powerful, searchable view of outgoing inventory. Filter by Order ID, BOL, facility, customer, carrier, or even trailer number to instantly locate shipments. With key details like ship time, appointment windows, and total weight visible at a glance, it's never been easier to track what's leaving your facility and find when it took place.

Key Benefits

Streamlined Operations

Themis-Trace simplifies essential inventory tasks such as order processing, scheduling, and real-time tracking within a single, intuitive platform. Its self-service web portal provides maximum accessibility and ease of use for both teams and logistics partners.

By eliminating manual entry and replacing traditional systems, it reduces errors and administrative burden, allowing teams to focus on moving products efficiently. With real-time visibility into inventory levels and locations, warehouse staff and managers can make faster, more informed decisions that increase productivity and minimize delays throughout the operation.

Secure, Accessible

With Themis-Trace, your inventory data is protected by industry-leading cloud security. Built on the infrastructure of trusted cloud providers, the system ensures all information is encrypted, safely stored, and backed up—shielding your operation from data loss or breaches.

Role-based access and secure login protocols keep control in the right hands, while remote accessibility empowers teams to manage inventory from anywhere without compromising security.

Effortless Check-Ins For Drivers

Our self check-in kiosk streamlines the arrival process for logistics partners. Upon arrival, drivers simply enter key details such as their truck number, trailer number, and Appointment ID.

This quick and intuitive process reduces wait times, eliminates bottlenecks, and provides the facility with real-time visibility into incoming shipments—helping everyone stay on schedule and reducing costly idling time.

Key Features

Invaluable Insights

Themis-Trace makes inventory management effortless—helping you easily receive, easily ship, and always know what's on the shop floor. Built-in tools for quality control, performance monitoring, and real-time reporting give your team the clarity they need to keep operations moving without the usual friction.

Quality Control & Compliance

Themis-Trace helps you uphold high standards by embedding quality checks directly into the receiving process—ensuring every item meets your specifications before it enters inventory. With automatic audit trails that log every movement and adjustment, you gain full transparency across your operation. This not only strengthens internal accountability but also supports compliance with industry regulations and customer requirements, giving you confidence in every transaction.

Simplify Shipping & Receiving

Themis-Trace takes the guesswork out of shipping and receiving by guiding teams through every step, from order creation to label printing and truck coordination. Real-time updates and intuitive task scheduling keep things moving smoothly, while self-service kiosks let drivers check in, print paperwork, and get back on the road faster. The result? Shorter wait times, fewer errors, and a more organized, efficient flow of goods in and out of your facility.

Plan To Scan

With Themis-Trace and CareGo's scanning capabilities, inventory is instantly logged into the system, making it fully traceable, trackable, and visible from the moment it arrives. No more searching the shop floor or second-guessing where a product is. Themis-Trace works seamlessly with most existing scanning devices, allowing teams to streamline workflows without overhauling equipment. It's a faster, smarter way to bring accuracy and control to your inventory management.

Customized Reporting

Themis-Trace makes it easy to create custom reports tailored to your operation's needs. Whether you're highlighting shipment history, inventory accuracy, or turnaround times, you can build reports that showcase performance in real terms. Share them directly with customers to provide transparency, build trust, and demonstrate just how smoothly your facility is running. It's a powerful way to turn data into a competitive edge.

Integration Station

Themis-Trace is built to work with the systems you already rely on. With robust API support and seamless integration capabilities, it ensures smooth data flow between platforms and eliminates silos and manual transfers. Whether connecting to your ERP or other existing tools, Themis-Trace keeps everything in sync so your operation runs smarter, not harder.

Multi-Platform

Stay connected to your operation from anywhere. Themis-Trace is fully accessible through its secure web portal, giving you real-time access to inventory, orders, and reports across devices. Whether you're on a desktop in the office, a tablet on the floor, or your phone on the go, Themis-Trace keeps your entire operation at your fingertips—no downloads, no hassle.
