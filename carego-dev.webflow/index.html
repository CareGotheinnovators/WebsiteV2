Precision Automation

We’ve been crafting and perfecting TELIA, our ground-breaking precision automation software to help facilities unlock their true potential. TELIA enables your lifts to execute remarkably precise movements, navigating even the most minute adjustments with effortless efficiency allowing for densely packed storage areas. With movements calibrated to the millimeter, TELIA is able to move and place products without damage every time. 

Safety

TELIA’s #1 priority is safety and by integrating our software with new or existing infrastructure, we monitor resources, sensors, gates and high-traffic areas. This creates a robust safety net for not only your operators and facility, but your product as well. Through real-time monitoring and automatic shutdown mechanisms, we prioritize operator safety, fostering a secure and efficient workplace environment.

Configure

One of TELIA’s hallmarks is it’s adaptability. TELIA is able to integrate into any facility with ease, allowing you to future-proof your building with existing equipment. TELIA’s configurable nature puts you into the driver’s seat to scale your operation head and shoulders above where you thought it could be. Upon installation, if you’re looking to remap your storage bays, just let TELIA know and it’ll be done without breaking a sweat.

Efficiency

Experience the leaps and bounds of operational efficiency gains, once thought impossible, with TELIA's ability to work around the clock. Engineered for precision and seamless performance, our solution eliminates wasted movements and streamlines every process. From optimized workflows to resourceful task execution, we can unlock a new era of productivity where efficiency is not just our goal, but our standard.

Storage

TELIA will revolutionize your existing space and take your storage bays to new heights. Unleash the potential to store up to 300% more product efficiently, strategically and securely. TELIA optimizes your space utilization, enhances inventory management and transforms your bays into highly productive spaces. It’s a complete paradigm shift in efficiency, empowering you and your company to thrive for years to come.

Dashboard

Empower your workforce with our Dashboard and get accurate updates on your throughput, workflow, inventory, and much more. Designed to streamline operations and enhance decision-making, our intuitive interface makes interacting with TELIA a breeze. Transform the way your team engages with its work, make informed decisions effortlessly through the Dashboard, and open the gateway to a smarter facility.

The Journey, from start to finish

Discovery

We’ll be embarking on a journey to automate your facility and this is where it all starts. Think of this stage as a casual chat in a coffee shop - just a conversation about goals, expectations, how your facility operates and how we can effectively use our technology to make improvements.

Deep Dive

As we move forward, this next step involves taking a deep dive into your process and facility. We’re looking to understand your pain points and where we can help you right away. Through transparent discussion, we’ll be able to pave the way for seamless integration and synchronization. With a shared commitment to excellence, this thorough exploration will become the foundation on which we craft an operation that reaches it’s potential everyday.

Concepting

This is where the vision for your facility really starts coming to life. With our team of designers and engineers, we’ll be able to draft concepts that shape the project going forward. We’ll create a virtual rendering of what your project will be, along with very detailed floor plans and product flows. After reviewing and sorting out details, we’ll be ready to start implementation.

Implementation

With the insights gained from our deep dives and concepting, the implementation phase becomes an exciting step toward turning our plans into action. Equipped with a clear understanding of our shared goals, we’re able to execute. This is where the magic happens - translating our collaborative sessions into tangible results. We’ll be working closely with you to ensure quality and agility in every process. 

Optimization

In the journey toward complete automation, the fifth step is dedicated to refining your operations. Leveraging data-driven insights via Power BI™ and our Dashboard's comprehensive reporting suite, we meticulously refine and polish every aspect of your workflow. Our aim is to optimize your operations to achieve the highest levels of efficiency and productivity.

Success

As we step into the success phase of our partnership, our commitment extends beyond the deployment of software - it encompasses a dedicated effort to empower and support the members of your team to get the most out of TELIA and it’s features.
