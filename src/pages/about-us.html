The Story of CareGo

Mission Statement:

We’ve been a company inspired by passion and dreaming of a better way. Led by an attitude of innovation and craftsmanship, we’ve cultivated a history of excellence through these two closely held ideals. There’s been ups and downs but through it all, we’ve never given up on the thought that any big problem can be solved with a bigger solution.

History

Years ago, we embarked on a journey of innovation and excellence. Read through our company’s rich history, as it unfolds through decades of pioneering, dedication, and transformative achievements.

1999

Sketch on a Napkin

In the 1980s, our founder and CEO, Demetrius Tsafaridis, spent over a decade at Dofasco, mastering the steel industry through a wide range of roles. His relentless drive and passion for new challenges led him to an insightful conversation with a colleague about steel warehousing during a trip. This sparked an idea in Demetrius’ mind. Combining his deep industry knowledge and entrepreneurial spirit, he saw a unique opportunity to build something new. Demetrius left his secure position and, along with his colleague, founded Thompson Storage, setting the foundation for what would become CareGo.

2000

Growing Strong

Two years into the journey, Demetrius' entrepreneurial vision evolved. He launched SteelCare, a 150,000-square-foot operation specializing in high-quality storage and transloading services. His drive for expansion continued with the creation of RailCare, focusing on rail car maintenance, and TransCare, which operated a fleet of 18 semi-trucks and 54 steel-haul trailers for transportation services. With a talented team in place, CareGo began its evolution into a diversified logistics powerhouse, effectively managing products across all transportation channels.

2003

Plant 19

With the businesses running smoothly, Demetrius sought to take the next bold step. He set out to create a next-generation, fully automated facility, while pursuing LEED Gold certification for sustainability. This innovative design allowed for more products to be stored in a smaller footprint, drastically reducing human interaction and enhancing safety and efficiency. Plant 19 became the benchmark for CareGo’s future—a testament to the company's commitment to pioneering new solutions in the logistics industry.

2009

Big Moves

Amid the global recession of 2008, Demetrius made strategic moves to streamline CareGo’s growing operations. TransCare, RailCare, and SteelCare were consolidated into a unified company, CareGo. By recognizing inefficiencies in rail transport, CareGo invented a cradle to support steel coils—a product that would have international impact. This innovation not only helped CareGo navigate the recession but also solidified the company’s reputation for commitment to industry-leading solutions.

2013

An Automation Sensation

By the time Plant 19 had proven successful, CareGo had been developing cutting-edge automation software—TELIA. TELIA revolutionized production and storage processes, leading the way for a new era of innovation in logistics. As CareGo shifted its focus to software development, the company’s deep industry experience, combined with its knack for problem-solving, made them leaders in automation. The demand for TELIA exceeded expectations, prompting a swift expansion to meet industry needs. With rapid growth, the CareGo team continued to push the envelope, revolutionizing the industry with every advancement.

2015

Innovation & Beyond

Demetrius sold off CareGo’s distribution business to focus entirely on technology. Since the successful proof of concept at Plant 19 over a decade ago, which showcased the transformative potential of automation in industrial storage and material handling, CareGo has remained dedicated to advancing its solution. Branded as TELIA, the platform has steadily evolved with regular enhancements. In 2015, the release of TELIA 2 marked a major milestone, introducing features to meet the diverse demands of facilities and product types, including long products and cassettes. This launch reinforced CareGo’s commitment to innovation, cementing its role in reshaping the industry’s future.

2018

Word Travels Fast

By 2018, TELIA had been integrated into over a dozen facilities across multiple countries, cementing its status as an industry leader. As word spread about CareGo’s ability to optimise facility operations, demand surged, driving our mission to expand TELIA’s reach. At the same time, we introduced groundbreaking features, including dual crane flight paths. This marked the start of a new era for CareGo—one of rapid growth, new product offerings, and entry into new markets, heralding an extraordinary journey filled with possibilities.

2020

Onwards, Upwards

The late 2010s saw CareGo experience significant growth, capped by CEO Demetrius being honored with the Hamilton Citizen of the Year Award. However, the pandemic presented unforeseen challenges, slowing down projects across the board. Rather than being deterred, we used this time to reflect, innovate, and improve. This led to the launch of TELIA 3, featuring over fifty new enhancements, and the introduction of TELIA Handheld, a cutting-edge inventory management system. Through resilience and collaboration, we not only navigated these uncertainties but emerged stronger, ready to thrive in any environment.

2021

Resistance & Persistence

During the pandemic’s unprecedented challenges, CareGo rose to the occasion, demonstrating resilience and determination. Drawing on a decade of growth and success, we quickly adapted by implementing upgrades to TELIA, enhancing functionality for our partners and streamlining workflows when efficiency was most critical. Undeterred by the obstacles, we achieved the milestone of integrating North America’s first coil magnet lift. These efforts strengthened our team’s culture of innovation and perseverance, preparing us to confidently tackle challenges ahead.

2022

A Renaissance

As we emerged from the pandemic, our confidence grew, driven by our commitment to improving material handling with TELIA 3. The smarter, faster platform enabled us to handle greater workloads and help facilities worldwide gain a competitive edge. While celebrating our achievements, CareGo stayed focused on enhancing TELIA’s capabilities and tackling challenges with tailored solutions as we expanded into new markets. Proud innovators with five patents and more pending, we continue to push the limits of excellence.

2025

The Road Ahead

As we look to 2025, it's an exciting time to reflect on our journey and the possibilities ahead. With our new logo and updated website, we are ready to make a meaningful impact. Behind the scenes, we’re working on transformative projects that will shape our future for years to come. TELIA for rectangular and palletized products is one example of our commitment to innovation, along with our new Vision automatic product detection and other cutting-edge solutions. As we continue to move forward, making a positive difference in our community remains central to our mission. The road ahead is full of opportunities, and we’re eager to continue this journey with you into the future.

Here We Stand

CareGo stands at the forefront of innovation, leading technological advancements in material handling optimization. Driven by pride and passion, we eagerly anticipate the transformative impact we will have on industries worldwide.

Join us on this exhilarating journey as we shape the future of technology and innovation for decades to come. Stay tuned for what lies ahead — an exciting wave of innovation that promises to redefine the material handling landscape.

Research & Development:

As a company that lives and breathes through our innovative ideas, we’re constantly looking for the next big thing.

Over the years, we’ve allocated the necessary resources into finding the next service that’s going to help our clients, and ourselves, grow to new heights. Ever since our outset, we’ve strived to be the creative problem solvers, people that pick the big fight and come out stronger because of it. With five patents, more pending, and even more in production, we’re far from finishing blazing a trail in this industry and the next ones we find ourselves in.

Community

We’ve always looked to make positive change in our industry and outside of it. One cause near and dear to us is the Eva Rothwell Center in Hamilton, Ontario; which helps families get the resources they need to be self-sufficient.

Our involvement has centered around education and health for the kids and families that count on the center’s support. We’ve restored an old train’s dining car and turned it into a reading sanctuary where kids can learn about the world around them. Along with the train, we’ve also revamped a piper airplane where kids can learn what it takes to become a pilot in an immersive environment. In our relationship with the center, we’ve been able to help over 50 kids get to the collegiate level and give them opportunities to pursue their dreams.

We love the work that’s being done at the Eva Rothwell Center and will continue to host fundraisers, food and clothing drives to help those reach their potential. We hope to positively impact many lives in our community for years to come and are always looking to empower the next generation.
